# Reproducibility and Research Quality

Use this skill as the primary operating playbook for this domain.

## Domain Focus
- Protocol and code traceability
- Versioned data and environment control
- Replication and audit readiness

## Workflow
1. Define hypothesis, variables, and what outcome would falsify it.
2. Select methods and sampling plan appropriate to the question.
3. Predefine analysis approach and quality controls before running.
4. Execute data collection and analysis with reproducible procedures.
5. Interpret results with uncertainty, limitations, and alternative explanations.
6. Translate insights into next experiments or product decisions.

## Deliverables
- Study plan with assumptions and controls
- Reproducible analysis workflow and results summary
- Interpretation memo with confidence and limitations
- Prioritized follow-up questions or experiments

## Quality Bar
- Tie recommendations to measurable outcomes and explicit constraints.
- Make tradeoffs explicit and name what is intentionally deprioritized.
- Keep assumptions testable and define what signal would invalidate the plan.
- Produce outputs another agent can execute without additional clarification.
