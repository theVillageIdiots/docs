# Goal Systems and OKRs

Use this skill as the primary operating playbook for this domain.

## Domain Focus
- Objective decomposition
- KR quality and anti-metrics
- Cadence for review and correction

## Workflow
1. Define the business objective, decision owner, and decision deadline.
2. Map current-state metrics and constraints from available evidence.
3. Develop 2 to 3 options with explicit tradeoffs and second-order effects.
4. Select the recommended path and document assumptions that can break it.
5. Translate the decision into milestones, owners, and review checkpoints.
6. Set escalation rules and a follow-up rhythm for fast correction.

## Deliverables
- Executive brief with recommendation and tradeoffs
- Prioritized action plan with owners and dates
- Risk register with mitigations and triggers
- Review cadence and success dashboard

## Quality Bar
- Tie recommendations to measurable outcomes and explicit constraints.
- Make tradeoffs explicit and name what is intentionally deprioritized.
- Keep assumptions testable and define what signal would invalidate the plan.
- Produce outputs another agent can execute without additional clarification.
