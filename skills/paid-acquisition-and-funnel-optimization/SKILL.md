# Paid Acquisition and Funnel Optimization

Use this skill as the primary operating playbook for this domain.

## Domain Focus
- Channel mix and bid strategy
- Creative and landing-page testing
- CAC payback and LTV governance

## Workflow
1. Define target audience, positioning angle, and conversion objective.
2. Build message hierarchy tied to audience pain and proof points.
3. Design channel strategy with creative, budget, and cadence.
4. Launch campaigns with clear instrumentation and experiment structure.
5. Analyze channel and funnel performance to reallocate spend quickly.
6. Document winning playbooks and retire underperforming tactics.

## Deliverables
- Campaign strategy brief and messaging map
- Channel plan with budget and KPI targets
- Creative testing matrix and execution calendar
- Performance readout with optimization actions

## Quality Bar
- Tie recommendations to measurable outcomes and explicit constraints.
- Make tradeoffs explicit and name what is intentionally deprioritized.
- Keep assumptions testable and define what signal would invalidate the plan.
- Produce outputs another agent can execute without additional clarification.
