# Sales Discovery and Qualification

Use this skill as the primary operating playbook for this domain.

## Domain Focus
- Discovery question architecture
- Qualification scorecards
- Pain impact and urgency mapping

## Workflow
1. Define account or customer context, objective, and success criteria.
2. Diagnose needs, blockers, and decision dynamics using structured discovery.
3. Map the best response play with value proof and stakeholder alignment.
4. Execute communication and follow-through with explicit commitments.
5. Track risk signals and trigger timely escalation or intervention.
6. Capture learnings to improve playbooks and handoffs across teams.

## Deliverables
- Account or case plan with next actions
- Value narrative tailored to stakeholder roles
- Risk and objection handling matrix
- Post-engagement lessons and process updates

## Quality Bar
- Tie recommendations to measurable outcomes and explicit constraints.
- Make tradeoffs explicit and name what is intentionally deprioritized.
- Keep assumptions testable and define what signal would invalidate the plan.
- Produce outputs another agent can execute without additional clarification.
